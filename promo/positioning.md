# Qwen Dictation Positioning

## Primary audience

Mac developers and local-AI users who type into Cursor, ChatGPT, Slack, mail,
browsers, and editors all day.

## Core promise

Hold Right Ctrl, speak, and Qwen Dictation types into the app you are already
using.

## Short descriptions

- Local-first Mac dictation powered by Qwen3-ASR.
- Push-to-talk dictation for any focused text field on macOS.
- A private, local alternative to cloud dictation for developers and power users.

## Main hooks

- Works in any app that accepts typing.
- Audio is processed locally on the Mac.
- Right Ctrl hold is faster than opening a separate transcription window.
- Vocabulary registration helps with names and specialist terms.
- Qwen3-ASR is the default local engine.

## Avoid

- Do not claim perfect accuracy.
- Do not imply medical, legal, or regulated transcription reliability.
- Do not say it replaces all dictation tools for everyone.
- Do not position it as an ad product or growth hack.
- Do not overstate privacy beyond local processing and current app behavior.

## Suggested repo description

Local-first macOS dictation powered by Qwen3-ASR. Hold Right Ctrl, speak, and type into any app.
